2048

(TWO-PLAYER)

Combine tiles to get to 2048 tile! to win. If timer is ON and finishes, player with the highest score wins the game. If both players can't move, game over!

HOW TO PLAY:

PLAYER ONE on the LEFT will use W: ↑ , A: ←, S: ↓, D: → to move the tiles.

PLAYER TWO on the RIGHT will use arrow keys . All tiles shifting in the same direction will combine into one.
